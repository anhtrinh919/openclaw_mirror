- Maximum deliberation (slowest/most expensive).
- Best at: complex reasoning, long dependency chains, tricky correctness.
- Best for: architecture decisions, complex investigations, “don’t miss anything” tasks.

Practical rule of thumb:
- **off/min** = speed
- **low** = default “careful helper”
- **medium/high** = when the cost of a mistake is high or the task is genuinely complex
